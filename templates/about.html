More About Swachh Bharat

Swachh Bharat Abhiyan is one of the largest cleanliness drives in the world, promoting a clean and hygienic India through citizen participation and technological innovation.

Workflow Information

  • Citizens can actively participate by uploading images of dirty places along with their live location.
  • This information is sent to the worker dashboard, where workers can address the issues.
  • After the work is completed, they provide feedback to the citizens.
  • Workers upload cleaned geo-tagged images to the admin for progress tracking.
  • Real-time monitoring ensures transparency and accountability in the cleaning process.

Future Goals

  • Implement advanced waste management systems across all cities
  • Increase public participation through awareness campaigns
  • Promote sustainable practices and recycling initiatives
  • Expand digital monitoring to rural areas
  • Develop smart waste collection systems using IoT technology

Impact So Far

  • Over 100 million toilets constructed nationwide
  • More than 60,000 villages declared open defecation free
  • Significant improvement in waste management systems
  • Increased public awareness about cleanliness and hygiene
  • Improved ranking in global cleanliness indices
